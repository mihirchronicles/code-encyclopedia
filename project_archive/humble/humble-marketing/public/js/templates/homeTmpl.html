Humble Pen


A designer pen made out of bamboo complimented with artist inspired stationery. Humble Pen's mission was to do good in the world by ethically manufacturing the product, giving artists a platform to share their work and supporting education for children in Haiti and Cambodia. For every purchase we donated money for educational expenses.
